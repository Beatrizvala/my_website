Visiting Portugal

About Portugal…

Portugal’s landscape is made of beaches and high mountains, but also of deep, verdant valleys and rolling hills. It is full of friendly people, cheap food and wine and it is really easy to travel around.

Where to go in Portugal…

If you are looking to go to the beach with a calm sea and enjoy the sun, the best place to go is Algarve, located in the south. And for surfers, looking for big waves Ericeira and Peniche are amazing places to visit and stay for a couple of days..

Lisbon is a beautiful and captivating city to visit. It is really traditional, but also full of modern architecture. Some of the go-to areas are Chiado, Terreiro do Paço, Alfama and Avenida da Liberdade.

Porto is almost the second capital of Portugal. There you can find the Ribeira, surrounded by colorful buildings rising up from the banks of the river Douro. Along Douro are located some of the world’s most incredible vineyards and doing a trip along the port wine route is one of the best parts of visiting Portugal.
